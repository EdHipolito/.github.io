This is our protoype thesis project, it is a student profile management system. With this mobile app, CIIT students will be able to see their schedules, past and current balances, history of grades, edit profile, floor plan of the school. The user will be notified,through a text or email, once they enter or leave the building. It is developed using React.js. All the data comes from Edusuite's React API.
